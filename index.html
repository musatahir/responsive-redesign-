About Bilal Masjid

Our masjid is a place of worship, learning, and community for Muslims in the Greater Portland area. We strive to foster unity, education, and outreach while upholding the values of Islam. We invite you to visit and experience our welcoming and diverse community. Scroll down to learn more!

Our Mission

Unity

Encouraging moderation, tolerance, and harmony within Islamic teachings.

Worship

Congregational prayers and religious events for all ages and genders.

Education

Providing Islamic educational programs for all ages and backgrounds.

Outreach

Building connections with Muslim and non-Muslim communities.

Our Story

Our Story

By 1995, the Muslim community had successfully acquired a small house, which was rezoned for use as a Masjid. Though modest in size, this space became the heart of the growing Muslim population in the area. It served as a place for daily prayers, Jumu'ah congregations, and small gatherings. Fundraising efforts began in earnest to expand the Masjid and provide better facilities for worshippers.
